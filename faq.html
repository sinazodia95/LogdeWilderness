Frequently Asked Questions

When is Check-in and Check-out at the Resort?
Check-in at the Lodge begins after 4 pm daily, if rooms are not ready upon arrival, guests are welcome to begin using the water park prior to check-in when operating hours are applicable. We recommend a smaller bag be packed with bathing suits and swim gear. Please leave all other remaining luggage locked in your vehicle until your room is ready. Upon arrival, visit the Front Desk to pick up your water park wristbands. Lockers, showers, and changing areas are available for use in the water park. Guests must check out of their room before 11 am on the day of their departure. Guests can enjoy the waterpark until close after checking out of their rooms. Late check-out packages are available on a limited basis and may be reserved for an additional fee. Must be arranged through our Reservations department at 518-824-6000 and will extend check-out until 2 pm.
How old do you have to be to check in or reserve a suite?
Guests must be at least 21 years of age (with valid ID) to reserve and check in to a suite. The guest under whose name the suite is booked must be present at the time of check-in.
Are there prohibited items not allowed at the lodge?
Below is a list of prohibited items not allowed on Six Flags property:
    Firearms, ammunition, knives, and weapons of any kind Mace/pepper spray, and other self-defense or restraining devices
    Marijuana (including marijuana enriched products) or any illegal substance
    Fireworks or other similarly explosive and/or flammable objects
    Spiked clothing/jewelry
    Lawn/folding chairs
    Glass bottles (excluding small containers such as baby food jars)
    Spray paint/markers
    Recreational devices such as drones, remote-controlled devices, laser pointers, scooters, skateboards, and shoes with built-in wheels
    Selfie sticks, monopods, and similar handheld extension poles for cameras or mobile devices
