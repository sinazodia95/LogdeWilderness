About The Lodge


Nestled on the embankment of the White iMfolozi River, Mthembu Lodge
offers each of its seven private suites and two-family units unprecedented
views of the pristine wilderness.

It is here at Mthembu, that you can experience
the wildlife up-close from the tranquility of your suite’s private deck.

An Authentic African Wildlife Experience


The Lodge is Wilderness is a luxury Private Game Reserve
found in the heart of KwaZulu-Natal’s Zululand.


A part of Africa that embodies the allure of the Big 5, the game drives,
the wilderness walks, the sheer luxury of its lodges, and the specialised
wellness treatments for every guest, from dawn to dusk.

Our guests are treated to a different experience every day, from our
dedicated and passionate staff and guides. Join us,
the The LODGE family, and discover the true spirit of Africa.
